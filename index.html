Biography

I am a fourth-year Computer Science PhD student in the Computer of Computing at Georgia Tech. I am interested in cryptography.
I graduated from Shanghai Jiao Tong University in 2017 (majored in Computer Science). I was a student of ACM Honored Program, Zhiyuan College at SJTU.

Research

I am now working with Professor Sasha Boldyerva, focusing on anonymous review system.
I worked with Professor Vlad Kolesnikov, also working on optimizations of some MPC protocols.
I worked with Professor Tom Ristenpart as an intern at Cornell Tech for half a year since August 2016.
