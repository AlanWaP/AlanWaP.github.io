Jiahui Lu

Biography

I am working as research scientist at JD.com Silicon Valley R&D Center on privacy computing.
I graduated from Georgia Institute of Technology in 2021 (M.S. in Computer Science).
I graduated from Shanghai Jiao Tong University in 2017 (B.S. in Computer Science). I was a student of ACM Honored Program, Zhiyuan College at SJTU.

Research

I worked with Professor Sasha Boldyerva, focusing on anonymous review system.
I worked with Professor Vlad Kolesnikov, also working on optimizations of some MPC protocols.
I worked with Professor Tom Ristenpart as an intern at Cornell Tech for half a year since August 2016.
