About Me

I am currently working as a software engineer at Cresta Intelligence Inc., focusing on backend integration.

Previously, I worked as a software engineer at Inspur USA Inc. on database system development, and as a research scientist at JD.com Silicon Valley R&D Center on privacy computing.

I graduated from Georgia Institute of Technology in 2021 with an M.S. in Computer Science.

I received my B.S. in Computer Science from Shanghai Jiao Tong University in 2017, where I was a student of the ACM Honored Program at Zhiyuan College.

Research

I worked with Professor Alexandra Boldyreva, focusing on anonymous review systems.

I worked with Professor Vladimir Kolesnikov, working on optimizations of Multi-Party Computation (MPC) protocols.

I worked with Professor Thomas Ristenpart as an intern at Cornell Tech for half a year starting August 2016.
